If you've stumbled upon this site, it's quite likely that you enjoy games related to Doom, Quake, Half-Life, or Half-life 2. What's more, you're probably also involved in creating content for either them, or one of their offsprings.

Doom, Quake, Goldsrc and Source have been amongs the most widely celebrated game engines through out the years, and are still being used as a creative outlet by an admirable number of people to this day.

Coders, Mappers and Modelers are each working away to create something fun and interesting. So why not bring these people together?

The BSP Club is here for you to showcase your progress, ask for help, or simply share your enthusiasm. Just hop in to our Discord server, and say hi.
